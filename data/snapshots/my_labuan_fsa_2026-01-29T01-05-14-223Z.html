Who we are

Labuan FSA is the regulatory authority overseeing the Labuan IBFC

It ensures that businesses operating within the jurisdiction comply with global standards, providing a secure and competitive environment for international trade and finance. The authority plays a pivotal role in promoting transparency, financial stability, and innovation within the region.
